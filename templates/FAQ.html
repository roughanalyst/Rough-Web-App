How to use NNS and NNS AUDIT page?

On the NNS page, start by picking a lot number. Once you've selected one, all the info related to that lot number will show up in a table. Then, you can filter the table just by clicking on the column headers.

When you click on the "NNS Audit" button, you'll land on the NNS Audit page. Here, you can filter and audit your data. On this page, you'll find the average, which comes in two types: new and old averages. After you finish auditing, simply click on the "Done" button to update your portfolio.

What is the significance of "Program" in this data?

"Program" refers to special orders of polished goods based on specific shape or 4C parameter requirement.

What does the "Article" attribute indicate in the data?

The "Article" attribute indicates rough diamonds based on size, original shape, clarity, and color for better organization, you have to first select this filter to insert any data in Report.

What does "Rough Cts" signify within this data?

"Rough Cts" represents the total original carat weight of rough diamonds in a lot.

What is the significance of the "Tag" attribute?

"Tag" is a label used to identify a part of the original single diamond.

What are "By Products" in this data?

"By Products" indicate the monetary estimation of leftover uncut or rough diamonds.
