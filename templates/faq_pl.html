QUESTIONS?

What is P & L Report ?

A P & L Report (Profit and Loss Report) summarizes a company's revenues, costs, and expenses over a specific period to show profit or loss.

What is "Polish Cts" field in P & L Report?

Indicates the carat weight of Polished Diamonds for Manufacturing.

What does the "COGS" field indicate in the report?

Cost of Goods Sold Means Direct costs of producing goods.

What does the "Expense" attribute indicate in the report?

The "Expense" attribute in Report indicates the total costs incurred by the company to operate its business, excluding the Cost of Goods Sold (COGS). This includes costs such as salaries, rent, utilities, marketing, and other operating expenses.

What does the "Production Cost" field indicate in the report?

The cost of raw materials and components used in the manufacturing process cost.

What does the "Provisonal" field indicate in the report?

The "Provisional" field in Report indicates estimated or temporary value that are not yet finalized by bombay team.These values are subject to change once final data is available and verified.

What does the "Avg Lock" attribute indicate in the report?

The "AVG LOCK" attribute indicates average price at which goods are sold or secured for sale, This price is agreed upon in advance and helps the company lock in revenue and manage price fluctuations.

What does the "Total Sales Value" attribute indicate in the report?

The "Total Sales Value" attribute in Report indicates the total revenue generated from the sale of goods.

What does the "Net Profit (Loss)" attribute indicate in the report?

The "Net Profit (Loss)" attribute in Report indicates the total profit or loss result after all revenues, costs, and expenses, including interest, have been deducted.
